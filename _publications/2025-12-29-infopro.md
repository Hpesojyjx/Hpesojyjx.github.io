This work proposes **InfoPro**, a locally supervised deep learning framework that maximizes information propagation to improve information flow and feature expression, leading to stronger generalization and training efficiency across multiple mainstream datasets.

My contribution focused on independently conducting multiple experiments and systematically validating the improvements in generalization and training efficiency.
