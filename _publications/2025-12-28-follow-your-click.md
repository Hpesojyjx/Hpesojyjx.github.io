This paper proposes **Follow-Your-Click**, an open-domain regional image animation approach that generates animations for arbitrary image regions through motion prompts and mouse clicks, improving both controllability and realism.

My contribution focused on conducting experiments and validating improvements in interactive experience and animation quality.
